Hadoop 2.7.7 Release Notes

These release notes include new developer and user-facing incompatibilities, features, and major improvements.

Changes since Hadoop 2.7.6

  • HDFS-13602. Major improvement reported by Erik Krogen and fixed by Chao Sun (ha , namenode)
    Add checkOperation(WRITE) checks in FSNamesystem
  • HDFS-13486. Major bug reported by Wei-Chiu Chuang and fixed by Wei-Chiu Chuang
    Backport HDFS-11817 (A faulty node can cause a lease leak and NPE on accessing data) to branch-2.7
  • HDFS-12156. Major bug reported by Akira Ajisaka and fixed by Akira Ajisaka (test)
    TestFSImage fails without -Pnative
  • HADOOP-15486. Major improvement reported by Nanda kumar and fixed by Nanda kumar (net)
    Make NetworkTopology#netLock fair
  • HADOOP-15473. Critical bug reported by Gabor Bota and fixed by Gabor Bota (kms)
    Configure serialFilter in KeyProvider to avoid UnrecoverableKeyException caused by JDK-8189997
  • HADOOP-14970. Minor bug reported by Erik Krogen and fixed by Erik Krogen
    MiniHadoopClusterManager doesn't respect lack of format option
